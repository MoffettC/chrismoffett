About

I believe at the root of all engineering is that your solving problems. The root of solving problems is curiosity and creativity. Since my first programming I did in middle school all the way to the present I have delved into many different languages over the years, always in a journey to become a better software developer. It is a journey that I dont ever plan to finish. Besides being incredibly passionate for software engineering, I love travelling, painting, and learning anything novel. And if you cant find me doing any of these things, Im probably on the beach with some friends.
